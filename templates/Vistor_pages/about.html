Biographies

Marcus Bussey (PhD) is a Senior Lecturer in History and Futures at the University of the Sunshine Coast, Australia. He has a keen interest in anticipatory aesthetics and phenomenologies of grace.

Cecilia Kristoffersson is an artist and art educator born and raised in Zimbabwe, now living in Southern Sweden. Through her paintings, Cecilia aims to foster a dialogue of a new female revolution that anchors itself in our understanding of our spiritual DNA and experiences. Experiences that bond grandmothers, mothers and daughters in the pathway of life; past, future and present.

Abstract

This poem is a reflection on the contradictions inherent to futures work. Pasts, presents and futures fold in on each other and this is reflected in the deliberately tribal image offered by Kristoffersson. In both image and poem lies biography and memory both personal and also epochal. Time is sticky and we must deal with that as best we can.
